Score of Peony Pavillion 牡丹亭曲譜

[Ming] Tang Xianzu [明]湯顯祖
Shanghai 1921 (facsimile)
Harvard-Yenching Library, 5658 2433.4

First performed in 1598 at the Pavilion of Prince Teng, The Peony Pavilion, adapted from a work by Ming-dynasty playwright Tang Xianzu, is a cornerstone of the Kun opera repertory.
